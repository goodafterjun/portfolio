My Philosophy

Understand the user.

Putting yourself in the user’s shoes is the best way to align with their needs. There’s no use in an end-product that a user can’t use.

Collaborate seamlessly.

Engineers and developers are the wonderful people who make our designs real. Knowing what they can and can’t do reduces frustration in the design process.

Anything that can be done should be done well.

Without sacrificing efficiency, I make sure that quality work is there every step of the way.

Cohesiveness is key.

Consistency matters, which is why I strive to create designs that are consistently enjoyable to use. The only surprises we’ll have are the desirable kind.
