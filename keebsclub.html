KEEBS CLUB AT UCI

Creating a hobbyist space
for the tech nerds.

A place for mechanical keyboard enthusiasts to connect.

Date

09/2022 - 04/2023

Client

Keebs Club at UCI

Role(s)

Project Lead
UI Designer

Concept

UC Irvine's first mechanical keyboard club, Keebs Club at UCI is fresh and innovative. Hosting county-wide keyboard meets, workshops and student-led projects, this club needed a website to match all the wonderful things they were cooking up. The old design was relic of 2000s design trends and lacked usability to easily access events and project information.

The club needed a website that was friendly, modern, and displayed all their cool projects.

Process

  • structuring the site.

    Keebs Club's website needed an intuitive structure that displayed the club overview and allowed potential members to join or access upcoming events. I strived for a clean, legible design with a simple layout. Animated buttons gave the website more flair.

  • Polishing the design.

    Keebs Club wanted to emphasize friendliness and approachableness without sacrificing a modern look. Rounded shapes gave the site a welcoming look and the color palette is simple. I also made sure there was plenty of space for the club's gorgeous project images.
